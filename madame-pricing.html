Questions
Everything you might wonder about before joining
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, always. No questions asked, no guilt from Madame. You can cancel from your account settings at any time. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
Is Madame Richi a real person?
Madame Richi is a carefully crafted AI persona — not a human, but not a generic chatbot either. Her voice, her stories, and her wisdom are built from real feminine wisdom, psychology, and a deep understanding of love. She will always tell you honestly when you ask if she is AI.
Is my conversation private?
Your conversations are encrypted and private. We do not read them, sell them, or use them for advertising. Your story belongs to you.
What is the difference between the Confidante and Daughter tiers?
Confidante gives you full access to everything Madame offers. Daughter adds unlimited voice calls (vs 30 min/week), priority response in busy periods, unlimited Decode Him, a monthly personal audio letter from Madame written about you, and access to The Salon — a private community of women in the inner circle.
Can I switch between plans?
Yes. Upgrade anytime — your new features activate immediately. Downgrade at the end of your billing cycle. Your conversation history and memories are always preserved regardless of plan.
