His attachment style
Avoidant tendency
Keeps things vague and non-committal. Responds warmly but never initiates clarity. The "I'll let you know" without a time — this is avoidant language.
What Madame noticed
Positive signal
He initiated with "had a really good time" — this is genuine. Men who are not interested do not send this.
Red flag
"Let's figure something out" and "I'll let you know" — both are non-commitments. He accepted your availability but made no plan. This is important.
Watch for
You offered your weekend. He said "cool." The power shifted in that moment. He is now in control of whether this happens.
Madame's verdict
"He is interested but comfortable. Comfortable men do not pursue — they wait to be convenient.

Three days of silence after this conversation tells you one thing: he is not thinking about you the way you are thinking about him. Not yet.

This is not a reason to walk away. It is a reason to stop making yourself so available."
What to do
Live your life visibly. Let him wonder if you are still available. One week of silence from your side will tell you everything.
What not to do
Do not text first. Do not suggest another time. Do not make it easier for him to stay comfortable.
